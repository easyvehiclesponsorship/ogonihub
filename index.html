About OGONI HR HUB

OGONI HR HUB is a digital data bank and workforce profiling system designed to serve the Ogoni communities. We connect skilled professionals, artisans, and workers with employers while creating accessible registration pathways for everyone.

Our Mission

This platform serves as a centralized profiling system for artisans, skilled workers, and professionals in Ogoni. Our goal is to connect these individuals to employers while creating community registration centres for those without digital access.

What We Do

    Build a trusted digital database for Ogoni artisans and professionals

    Enable employers to easily access verified profiles

    Empower communities with offline registration centres

    Create a scalable platform for workforce visibility
